FutureFinder

FutureFinder is a mobile application that helps high-school students explore careers based on their personality, interests, and workstyles.

View Prototype

Opportunity

Core Features

FutureFinder consists of three primary features: Daily Questions, Daily Job Cards, and the Profile. Each of these features plays a role in helping users assess their career preferences and providing the system with relevant information to tailor content to users.

Daily Questions

Each day, a set of ten questions asses students’ preferred work styles, drives, interests, and career personality.

Daily Job Cards

Students then receive five job cards relating to their preferences. Students can read more about each job and to favorite the jobs in which they are most interested.

Profile

The profile allows students to view the preferences that the system has learned about them in terms of interests, workstyles, drives, and personalities.
